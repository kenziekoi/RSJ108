How to Download Fonts for Photoshop

Assembly time: 10 minutes
Awesome rating: 90% awesome

Are you looking for a fresh font to use that Adobe doesn't have? Look no further!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Mac-Based Device
  • Access to Font Website
  • Adobe Cloud/Photoshop
  • Patience

  1. Find and download your new font.

    Search online for Mac-friendly fonts that are free to download and use. When prompted, download the fonts to your desktop for easy access.

  2. Close active tabs.

    Many programs on the Mac have font support, so applications will search for fonts. Closing active applications will allow you to download the new font before your computer searches for it.

  3. Open the font in Font Book.

    After opening Finder, you will find the font in a ZIP folder. Double-click to open. From there, simply double-click on the actual font to open it in your Font Book. The font will either have .ttf or .otf at the end.

  4. Install the font.

    When Font Book appears after you open your .ttf or .otf file, you can click “Install Font” in the bottom left corner to install to the Mac. This font will now be accessible in any program on the Mac that has fonts.

  5. Open your new font in Adobe Photoshop.

    During your next project, use the character tab to search for and use your new font. Voilà!
